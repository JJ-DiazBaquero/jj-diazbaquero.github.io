    Portfolio Optimization

    Optimization Framework and visualization for the portfolio optimization problem in S&P500

    This project was develop for the INFORMS OR & ANALYTICS Student Team Competition, with my team we reached the 8 finalists. Then presented our solution in the informs business analytics conference in Baltimore and reached 2nd place.

    The problem was to develop an optimization framework that solves the portfolio optimization problem under time and non-linear constraints. We used a master-slave approach, mixing genetic algorithms in R, and non-linear exact optimization solvers in Julia.

    Finaly I created a visualization tool in R (framework Shiny) to explore the results and give insight of the portfolio performance through time

    • Date: April 2018
    • Link: 2018orastc171.shinyapps.io/PortfolioViz/
    • Category: Business Analytics / Information Visualization

    Ciclovia Program

    Simulation to estimate the number of cyclist in the Ciclovia

    This was my bachelor's thesis, it is a simulation implemented in django in the language Python, it models a medium sized ciclovia, ciclovias are a type of social program in which in a single day the government of a city closes some main streets, and people can come out their homes and ride bicycles or do exercise outside.

    Ciclovias are an open system in which people can enter or get out whenever they want, this makes the system very hard to measure, which is important in order to evaluate the health impact in the population

    The program is intended to be free to any ciclovia program in the world, we also created a parallel simulation in a commercial simulation software in order to compare both simulation results

    • Date: December 2015
    • Category: Data Science

    Hogwarts students Viz

    Web visualization of Harry Potter's characters

    This is a visualization I create while I learn D3 javascript library. The data comes from pottermore.com, I started with a web crawler built in R that automatically explore the web page and downloaded the information of each character, including name, affiliation, description and picture.

    German common words explorer

    Analyzing German language through the most common words

    This was developed with data from Das Institut für Deutsche Sprache, my problem is to learn lots of new vocabulary, but I didn't know which words would be most useful to start learning. So I decided to create an app that allowed me to identify and prioritize words for me to learn.

    Everything is made with R, I use the framework Shiny to make it web and shinyapps.io to host it, I also use the API of openthesaurus.com that shows me related terms and examples of a certain word.

    If you want to help me make it better, or have a cool idea of what can I do to make it cooler, feel free to contact me, I have not yet uploaded the source code, but I will in the near future. My intention is it to be free and open for everybody.

    PSDT: If it its useful for you, you can play with the demo :)

    • Date: Since June 2018
    • Demo: aire.shinyapps.io/vzGermanCommonWords/
    • Data Source: www1.ids-mannheim.de/kl/projekte/methoden/derewo.html
    • Category: Data Visualization
